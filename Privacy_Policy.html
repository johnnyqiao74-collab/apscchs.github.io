Privacy Policy

Last Updated: January 2026

1. Data Collection

We collect personal information (such as name and contact info) only when you voluntarily submit it via our "Get Support," "Feedback," or interest forms.

2. Use of Information

Your information is used to provide peer support, organize club events, and improve our services. We do not sell your personal data to third parties.

3. Confidentiality & Mandatory Reporting

Privacy Limit: While we respect your privacy, we are Mandatory Reporters under MCPS policy.

If you disclose information regarding intent to harm yourself or others, or report abuse/neglect, we are legally required to notify school administration or relevant authorities immediately.

4. Data Security

We implement reasonable measures to protect your data. However, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure. By using our forms, you acknowledge this inherent risk.

5. External Links

Our website contains links to external sites (e.g., Instagram, DoubleGood). We are not responsible for the privacy practices of these third-party platforms.
