About Our Club

The Asian Peer Support Club (APSC) at Clarksburg High School is dedicated to fostering a supportive community for Asian students. Our mission is to provide a safe space for sharing experiences, cultural exchange, and personal growth.

We organize regular meetings, workshops, and social events to promote understanding and collaboration among members.
