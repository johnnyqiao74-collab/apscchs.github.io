About APSC

The Asian Peer Support Club (APSC) is a student-led organization dedicated to supporting Asian and multicultural students while facilitating cross-cultural communication within our school community. Through leadership development, service opportunities, and educational initiatives, APSC provides meaningful experiences for all members.

Eligibility Notice

President and Vice President applicants must be current APSC members and meet Active Member requirements:

  • ≥ 70% meeting attendance
  • ≥ 80% task completion
  • No disciplinary violations
